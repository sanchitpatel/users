# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Premium Apple device users in Punjab (Patiala) who need reliable, certified-quality third-party repairs. They want transparency, genuine parts, rapid turnaround (same-day), and expert precision.

## Product Purpose
A premium landing page for Applifix India (Apple Device Repair Center) to showcase repair expertise, build trust through reviews and video demonstrations, and drive booking conversions.

## Brand Personality
Professional, surgical, premium, trust-inspiring, expert. 

## Anti-references
AI-slop tells: neon glows, fake screenshots, generic illustrations, emoji-spammed reviews, vague taglines.

## Design Principles
- Show, don't tell: real YouTube repair videos and scroll-driven interactive steps instead of mock screenshots.
- Surgical precision: clean margins, glassmorphic refraction, absolute visual clarity.
- True premium feel: Outfit and Inter typography with a slate-grey studio backdrop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for body copy).
- Responsive layouts catering to all device viewports.
- Reduced motion fallback for parallax and scroll transitions.
